Universal Solving Strategies

If you’re stuck, do these before you doom-scroll hints.

1) Text & UI Audit (the game lies)

  • Tap the instruction text itself.
  • Drag the level number, menu button, hint bulb, and any “UI” you assume is untouchable.
  • Try moving or “fixing” a word if the sentence is the puzzle.

2) Touch Sweep (fast checks)

  • Tap everything once (including empty space).
  • Long-press suspicious elements.
  • Drag anything that looks static.
  • Pinch in / pinch out.
  • Use 2–4 fingers (some levels only respond to multi-touch).

3) Sensor Sweep (phone is the controller)

  • Tilt left/right/up/down.
  • Flip upside down.
  • Shake.
  • Blow into the mic, clap, or stay silent.
  • Cover the top bezel (light sensor area).
  • Put the phone flat and do nothing for a few seconds.

4) System Button Sweep (outside-the-app tricks)

  • Take a screenshot (common “camera” trick).
  • Use volume up/down.
  • Toggle airplane mode/connectivity if the level hints at signal/power.
  • Plug in a charger if it screams “energy.”
